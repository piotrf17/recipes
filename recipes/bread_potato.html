INGREDIENTS

  • 1 baking potato
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons lard (or butter)
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon dry yeast
  • 1 1/3 cup warm water
  • 5 cups flour

DIRECTIONS

  1. Boil potato until soft. Drain and reserve cooking liquid. Mash the potato.
  2. Scald milk (just to to boil), and combine with 1/2 cup cooking liquid.
  3. Mix mashed potato with lard, butter, salt, sugar and liquid. Set aside to cool.
  4. Combine yeast and warm water, wait till foamy. Add to cooled potato mixture.
  5. Add flour and knead for ~7 minutes.
  6. Transfer to buttered bowl, cover, and let rise for 1/2 hour.
  7. Punch down dough, knead briefly, and then cut in half and put into 2 9x5x5 inch loaf pans. Let rise for 45 minutes (until doubled).
  8. Bake for 30 minutes @ 350F.
