INGREDIENTS

  • 6-8 small potatoes
  • 2 cups lentils
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1/4 cup scallions
  • 3-4 cloves garlic
  • 1 large pepper
  • 2 medium tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • ~ masala, salt

DIRECTIONS

  1. Soak lentils in hot water for a few cycles
  2. Boil Potatoes till soft
  3. Sautee onions, scallions and garlic
  4. Boil lentils till soft
  5. Pour onion mix and spices into lentils
  6. Add tomato to lentil mix
  7. Cut up potatoes, pepper and mix with lentils
  8. Add coconut milk, spice to taste.
