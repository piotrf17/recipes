INGREDIENTS

  • 9 sheets lasagna pasta
  • a bunch of garlic
  • 1 medium/large onion
  • 3ish carrots
  • 8oz mushrooms
  • 12oz frozen spinach brick
  • 15oz ricotta (sold in yogurt-like containers)
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 cups grated mozzarella (just buy a bag)
  • 1/2-1 cups grated parmesan
  • 1 24oz jar of marinara sauce (i think used a basil spiced one)

DIRECTIONS

  1. start boiling pasta
  2. start frying chopped onion, carrots and garlic in olive oil, add salt and freshly ground black pepper, some italian seasoning, then add mushrooms. after 5-10 minutes add spinach and 1/4-1/3 jar of marinara
  3. mix ricotta with 2 eggs and some of the parmesan and mozzerella
  4. assemble lasagna:
    • lay down some sauce
    • put down 3 sheets of pasta
      • pour half the ricotta/egg mix
      • pour a bit of cheese
      • add half the onion/carrot/spinach mix
    • repeat above sequence
    • lay down 3 sheets of pasta
    • pour remainder of sauce (should be about 1/4 of it)
    • pour 2 cups of mozezarella for a nice cheesy top
  5. bake at 350 degrees, covered in aluminum foil for 40-50 minutes
  6. take off aluminum foil and continue baking until top cheese browns nicely
