INGREDIENTS

  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup uncooked long-grain rice
  • 1-2 cloves garlic (original: 1 teaspoon garlic salt)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 3/4-1 cup tomato sauce (original: 1/2)
  • 2 cups chicken broth

DIRECTIONS

  1. Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat and add rice. Cook, stirring constantly, until puffed and golden. While rice is cooking, sprinkle with salt and cumin.
  2. [MODIFICATION] add a bunch more spices, e.g. mexican oregano, paprika, red pepper flakes, chilli powder
  3. Stir in onions and cook until tender. Stir in tomato sauce and chicken broth; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cover and simmer for 20 to 25 minutes. Fluff with a fork.
