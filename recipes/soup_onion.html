INGREDIENTS

  • 6 large red or yellow onions
  • olive oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon of sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 cups of beef stock
  • 1/2 cup dry vermouth or dry white wine
  • 1/4 teaspoon of dry thyme
  • salt and pepper
  • 8 slices toasted French bread
  • 1 1/2 cups grated Swiss Gruyere + some grated Parmesan

DIRECTIONS

  1. Saute the onions in olive oil on medium high till browned (~30-40 minutes). Add sugar 10 minutes in to carmelize onions.
  2. Add garlic, saute for 1 minute. Add vermouth/wine, cook it off. Add stock, cook partially covered for ~30 minutes. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
  3. Ladle soup into bowls, cover with soap and sprinkle with cheese. Put in broiler for 10 minutes at 350 degrees F.
