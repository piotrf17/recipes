INGREDIENTS

  • 1 3/4 C Flour
  • 1/2 t Salt
  • 2 t Pumpkin Pie Spice
  • 1 t Baking Soda
  • 1/2 C Butter Softened
  • 1 C Sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 C Applesauce
  • 1/2 C Raisins
  • 2 C Uncooked Oatmeal

DIRECTIONS

  1. Stir together Flour,Salt, Pumpkin Pie Spice, & Baking Soda.
  2. Beat together until fluffy: Butter, Sugar, & Egg
  3. All at once, stir in flour mixture & applesauce. Beat well. Stir in raisins & oatmeal.
  4. Drop by teaspoons on greased cookie sheet. Bake at 350 - 375 for 10 to 15 minutes.
  5. Cool completely before storing.
