INGREDIENTS

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 stalk celery, chopped
  • 1-2 medium carrots, chopped
  • 1 medium potato, cubed
  • 1 medium/large butternut squash - peeled, seeded, and cubed
  • 1 (32 fl. oz.) container chicken stock
  • salt and black pepper to taste
  • (garlic)
  • (1/2 cup cream)

DIRECTIONS

  1. Melt butter in large pot, and cook vegetables 5 min. or until browned
  2. Pour enough chicken stock to cover vegetables, bring to a boil
  3. Reduce heat to low, cover pot, simmer for 40-60min. (until tender)
  4. Transfer soup to blender, and blend till smooth.
  5. Transfer back to pot, add remaining stock to correct consistency, season.
