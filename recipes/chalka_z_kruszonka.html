INGREDIENTS

  • 400g flour + 2 tbsp for crumb
  • 150g warm milk (110F) + 2 tbsp for wash
  • 60g sugar + 1 tbsp for crumb
  • 60g butter (melted and cooled) + 1 tbsp for crumb
  • 1 yolk + 1 egg + 1 white for wash
  • 10g active dry yeast
  • pinch of salt

DIRECTIONS

  1. Mix together yeast, 1 spoon sugar, 2 spoons flour, milk. Let sit covered for 15 minutes.
  2. Mix in the rest of the flour, sugar, salt, and the eggs.
  3. Mix in the melted butter, then knead for several minutes until smooth. Let sit covered for 30 minutes.
  4. Punch the dough down, knead lightly in bowl. Let sit covered for 30 minutes.
  5. Turn the dough out onto a surface, form a cylinder, and split into 3 pieces (cover).
  6. With each piece, make a cylinder approx 40cm long. Weave the cylinders together. Cover, and let sit for 15 minutes.
  7. While the bread is rising, mix the 2 tbsp flour, 1 tbsp sugar, 1 tbsp butter with your fingers to make a crumb.
  8. Once bread is risen, wash with 2 tbsp milk + 1 egg white, pour on crumb, and put into oven at 350F. Bake for around 35 minutes.
