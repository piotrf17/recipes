INGREDIENTS

  • 1.5 cup sugar
  • 1 cup oil
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1.5 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 3 cups shredded carrots
  • 1 cup chopped nuts

DIRECTIONS

Mix sugar, eggs, oil and beat for 1 minute. Add everything else except carrots and nuts and beat for 1 minute. Stir in carrots and nuts. If using 13 x 9, bake at 350 for 35-40 minutes. If using 2 8 1/2 inch round pans, bake for 40-45 minutes. If using 2 9 1/2 inch round pans, bake for 35-40 minutes.

Cream cheese frosting

INGREDIENTS

  • 16 oz cream cheese
  • 4 cups powder sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 tablespoon milk

DIRECTIONS

Beat cream cheese, vanilla, and milk then slowly add powdered sugar. You could probably omit the milk since I think the frosting might have been too runny. There was a little extra frosting, so a half batch would be enough if you only want to frost the top of the cake.
