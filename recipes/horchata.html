INGREDIENTS

  • 6 tablespoons rice
  • 1 1/4 cups blanched almonds (might be a little too much)
  • 1 inch cinnamon stick (~ 1 tsp)
  • some lime zest
  • 1 cup sugar (really, to taste)
  • 2 cups milk

DIRECTIONS

  1. Blend rice, almonds, lime zest.
  2. Stir in 2 1/4 cups hot water, mix, let sit overnight
  3. Blend mixture, and filter a few times through cheese cloth.
  4. Add 2 cups water, blend quickly again.
  5. Finally, add 2 cups milk, finish!
