INGREDIENTS

  • 1 pound mini shells pasta
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2 cups grated Cheddar-Jack
  • 8 ounces processed cheese, such as Velveeta, diced
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon seasoned salt
  • [MODIFICATION] 1/2 red onion
  • [MODIFICATOIN] red chili pepper flakes

DIRECTIONS

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package directions.
  2. [MODIFICATION] Sautee diced red onion
  3. Heat the milk and butter in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add the cheeses and stir until melted. Stir in the pepper, salt, seasoned salt, and red chili pepper flakes. Drain the pasta, and stir it into the cheese sauce. Serve immediately.
