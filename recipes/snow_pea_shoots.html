INGREDIENTS

  • 1 bag of snow pea shoots
  • 1-2 cloves garlic, minced
  • [optional] mirin or oyster sauces

DIRECTIONS

  • Wash and destem shoots
  • Add oil to a wok and turn to high heat. Add minced garlic and brown. Add pea shoots and cook till slightly wilted.2
  • Optionally add in mirin or oyster sauce, though these might have too strong flavor.
