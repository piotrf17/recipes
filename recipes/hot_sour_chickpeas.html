INGREDIENTS

  • 1 can chickpeas, drained
  • 2 medium onions, very finely chopped
  • 2 medium tomatoes, peeled and finely chopped
  • 1-2 hot green chilies, seeded and finely chopped
  • 1-inch fresh ginger, grated
  • 4 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
  • coriander, cumin, fenugreek, cinnamon

DIRECTIONS

  1. Heat onions (minus 2 tbsp.) in oil for 4-5 minutes on medium heat, till brown.
  2. Add tomatoes, cook for 5-6 minutes mashing into a pulp.
  3. Add spices, chickpeas, cover and simmer for 15-20 minutes. Add salt to taste.
  4. Mix reserved onion with chili, ginger and lemon juice.
  5. Add sauce mixture and cilantro into chickpeas, adjust seasoning and serve!
