Blend together into a smooth paste:

  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • ¾ cup chopped tomato
  • ½ inch ginger
  • 2-3 garlic
  • 1-2 green chilis
  • 10-12 cashews
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves
  • 4-5 black peppercorns
  • ½ inch cinnamon
  • 1 tsp coriander seeds
  • 2-3 cloves
  1. Saute ½ tsp cumin seeds in 2-2.5 tbsp ghee
  2. Add paste from above, simmer 10-12 minutes on medium heat until oil
  3. separates from paste
Add:
  • ¼ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp red chili powder
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • salt to taste
  1. add 1 cup frozen peas
  2. add water as needed until peas are cooked
  3. Add 250-300 grams diced paneer, cook 1-2 minutes
