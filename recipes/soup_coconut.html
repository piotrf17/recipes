INGREDIENTS

  • 3/4 pound boneless, skinless chicken (optional)
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil (optional)
  • Carrots + Onions (optional)
  • 2 (14oz) cans coconut milk
  • 2 cups water/chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons minced fresh ginger root
  • 4 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1/4-1/2 cup fresh lime juice / lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 2 tablespoons thinly sliced green onion
  • 2 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
  • More spices: red pepper, curry, soy sauce

DIRECTIONS

  1. Cut chicken into strips, saute in oil till both sides are white.
  2. Mix water and coconut milk in a pot, get it to boil.
  3. Add ginger, fish sauce, lime, cayenne pepper and chicken, simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Sprinkle with scallions and fresh cilantro, serve hot.
  5. Also good is to serve over rice vermicelli and fresh bean sprouts.
