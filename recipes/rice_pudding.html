INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 cup uncooked rice
  • water
  • cinnamon
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1-2 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar

DIRECTIONS

  1. Place rice and some cinnamon in a pot and cover with water. Bring to a boil and then simmer 15-20 minutes or until water is absorbed.
  2. Add coconut milk, regular milk, vanilla and brown sugar. Simmer 15-20 minutes.
  3. Add a bit of lemon juice (optional) or cinnamon/nutmeg to taste. Simmer until aalmost all water is absorbed.
