INGREDIENTS

  • 2 pounds cubed pork stew meat
  • 1 large yellow onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3-4 jalapenos
  • 1 yellow bell pepper
  • 1 pound fresh tomatillos
  • 1/2 bunch cilantro leaves
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 2-4 cups chicken broth
  • salt, pepper
  • flour

DIRECTIONS

  1. Dust the cubed pork in flour, and sear in olive oil in a large stock pot.
  2. Remove the pork from the pot, and cook the onion and garlic until tender.
  3. Season with cumin, salt, pepper, and then add in the pork and some of the chicken broth, simmer for 1/2 hour.
  4. Chop 2 jalapenos and bell pepper, stir into the mix.
  5. Puree the tomatillos, cilantro, and remaining jalapenos in a blender, and add them to the pot. Simmer for 1+ hours.
  6. Add any remaining chicken broth to achieve desired consistency.
