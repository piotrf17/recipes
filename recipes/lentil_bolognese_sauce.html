INGREDIENTS

  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 2 carrots, coarsely grated
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 2/3 cup red lentils
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 can (14 oz.) tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cups vegetable stock (or water)
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh marjoram or 1 tsp dried marjoram
  • salt and black pepper
  • olive oil

DIRECTIONS

  1. In a large saucepan, gently fry the onion, garlic, carrots and celery in the oil for about 5 minutes, until soft.
  2. Add lentils, tomatoes, tomato paste, stock, marjoram and seasoning.
  3. Bring mixture to a boil, then partially cover with a lid and simmer for 20 minutes, until thick and soft.
