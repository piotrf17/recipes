INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups gently packed basil leaves
  • 1 medium garlic clove, chopped
  • 1/4 cup toasted pine nuts
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated parmesan

DIRECTIONS

  1. Using your knife, finely chop the basil and pine nuts.
  2. Chop the garlic, sprinkle salt, then using the edge of the knife smear into a paste.
  3. Place in a small bowl, and add the oil until the mixture is shiny and moist.
  4. Stir in the cheese and season to taste with salt and pepper.
