INGREDIENTS

  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1/3 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup mashed bananas (~3)
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans (optional)
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips (optional)

DIRECTIONS

Preheat oven to 325, grease one 9x5 inch loaf pan.

Blend eggs, buttermilk, oil and bananas.

Sift sugar, flour, baking soda and salt. Add to banana mixture and stir well. Add vanilla and cinnamon. Add pecans and mix well.

Pour into loaf pan, and top with chocolate chips. Bake for 1:15-1:20.
