INGREDIENTS

  • 5 potatoes
  • 1.5-2 cups flour
  • 1 egg
  • salt

DIRECTIONS

  1. Boil potatoes, mash finely and let cool.
  2. Mix potatoes, flour and egg. Adjust flour to make a dough.
  3. On a floured surface, roll out cylinders to the thickness of your thumb.
  4. Cut slices 1" long, and throw into salted boiling water. Once floating, let cook for 2-4 minutes.
