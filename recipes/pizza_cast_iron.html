Dough

INGREDIENTS

  • 11 oz (2 cups + 2 tbsp) flour (00 pizza or bread)
  • 1 1/8 tsp yeast
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3/4 cup water at 110F

DIRECTIONS

  1. Add flour, yeast and salt to blender, pulse 5 times to mix.
  2. Add oil, turn blender on. Slowly pour in water and let blend for 30s. Let sit for 2 minutes, then blend for 30s more.
  3. (If too watery, add a bit of flour and blend for 10s more.)
  4. Knead on floured surface for roughly 1 minute.
  5. Place in oiled bowl, cover, and let sit for 1.5 hours.

Sauce

INGREDIENTS

  • 28oz can of whole tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp dried oregano

DIRECTIONS

  1. Strain juice out of tomatoes and reserve. Place tomatoes in blender.
  2. Add olive oil, cloves, vinegar, oregano and blend for 30s.
  3. Pour into a measuring cup, add reserved juice to reach 2 cups if necessary.

Neopolitan Pizza (x2)

INGREDIENTS

  • ~12oz fresh mozerella cheese
  • fresh basil

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat oven to 500F (or as close as it can get!)
  2. Brush 12" cast iron skillet with 1 tbsp olive oil
  3. Turn out dough onto floured counter top. Divide into 2.
  4. Press and then roll out dough into a skillet shaped circle, then place on skillet
  5. Spread tomato sauce on pizza, add slices of mozerella.
  6. Cook on stove top for 3-5 minutes, checking bottom to see if starting to become golden / cooked.
  7. Add to oven for 7-10 minutes, look for cheese to be burning / golden.
