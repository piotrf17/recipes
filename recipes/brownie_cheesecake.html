INGREDIENTS

  • 1 Pkg (13x9) Brownie Mix
  • 4 - 8 oz Pkg Cream Cheese - Softened
  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 1t vanilla
  • 1/2 Cup Sour Cream
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 Squares Semi Sweet Baking chocolate melted & cooled Slightly

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat Oven to 350 Line 13x9 " baking pan w foil w ends of foil extending over pan.
  2. Spray foil w cooking spray.
  3. Prepare Brownie Mix as directed on pkg & Pour into prepared pan. Bake 15 min or until top of brownies is shiny & center is almost set.
  4. Meanwhile ...Beat Cream Cheese, Sugar & Vanilla in large bowl w electric mixer on medium speed until well blended. Add Sour Cream mix well. Add eggs 1 at a time mixing on low speed after each addition just until blended. Pour batter over partially baked brownie in pan. Filling will come almost to top of pan.
  5. Bake 40 minutes until center is almost set. Run knife around the rim of pan to loosen cake. Cool. Refrigerate 4 hours or overnight. Let stand at room temp 30 minutes before serving. Lift Cheesecake from pan using foil handles. Drizzle with chocolate. Let stand until chocolate is firm. Cut into 16 pieces. Store in refrigerator.
