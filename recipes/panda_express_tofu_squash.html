INGREDIENTS

Stir Fry

  • 1 lb block of tofu
  • 3-4 squash / zucchini
  • (optional) mushrooms
  • 4 cloves of garlic

Sauce

  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1.5 tsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp brown sugar
  • 1.5 tsp cornstarch
  • 4 tbsp water

DIRECTIONS

Cut tofu into triangles. For a standard block, cut into 2 tall columns, then diagonally to make 4 triangular prisms. Then cut across into 6 pieces each, for 24 triangles total. Combine with oil and cornstarch, and air fry for 20 minutes at 400F.

Combine all sauce ingredients in a bowl.

Cut squash into ~1/2 inch pieces. Heat up oil in a wok to high temperature and throw in squash. Cook for 5-7 minutes until partially softened (but still retains some bite) and turning golden.

Add garlic to wok and cook for 1 minute more.

Add fried tofu and sauce, stir and cook for 1 minute more.
