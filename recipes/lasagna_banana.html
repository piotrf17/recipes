INGREDIENTS

  • 1 lb ground turkey
  • 1 onion, minced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 green pepper, minced
  • chopped cilantro
  • 2 tsp adobo (optional)
  • 2 tsp oregano
  • 2 tbs vinegar (optional)
  • 1 envelope sazon
  • 1/2 cup raisins (optional)
  • 1/4 cup tomato sauce
  • 4 plantains, peeled and sliced into strips
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tbs milk
  • 2 cups white shredded cheese

DIRECTIONS

  1. Heat a LARGE frying pan with oil, enough to coat the bottom. Fry plantains till golden and slightly crispy. This is the bottleneck!
  2. Combine beef, onion, pepper, garlic, cilantro, adobo, orgeano, vinegar and sazon. Mix well.
  3. Heat a skillet at medium-high heat with olive oil, add meat mixture.
  4. Cook until brown, add raisins and tomato sauce. Mix and let simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Assemble with layers of plantain, beef, cheese. Top off with plantains and cheese.
  6. Beat 3 eggs with milk, pour over lasagna, add a tiny bit more cheese.
  7. Bake in oven at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.
