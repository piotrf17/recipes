INGREDIENTS

  • Bacon
  • Chicken thigh
  • Pork
  • Cabbage
  • Dried mushrooms
  • Sauerkraut
  • Bay leaf, pepper, all spice

DIRECTIONS

  1. Start soaking the mushrooms in hot water.
  2. Fry the bacon in a big dutch oven.
  3. Add the chicken thigh and pork, cook till changes color.
  4. Add the cabbage, cook until starting to wilt a bit.
  5. Add mushrooms, sauerkraut (including liquid to flavor) and spices. Cover and cook for at least an hour.
